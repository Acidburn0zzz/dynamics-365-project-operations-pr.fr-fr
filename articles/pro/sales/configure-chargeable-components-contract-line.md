<span data-ttu-id="e585a-106">Les composants inclus sont des composants soumis à :</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e585a-106">Included components are components that are subject to:</span></span>

  - <span data-ttu-id="e585a-113">Non facturable</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e585a-113">Non-chargeable</span></span>
